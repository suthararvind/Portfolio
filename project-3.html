Project Overview

I designed and developed a robust Food App utilizing the MERN stack, encompassing MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js. Integrating Razorpay ensures secure payment processing for users. Google authentication offers convenient and secure access. The app boasts intuitive menu browsing, enabling users to effortlessly explore various options. Seamless order placement and tracking enhance user experience and satisfaction. Detailed order history provides users with insights into their past transactions. Additionally, an Admin dashboard empowers administrators with comprehensive control and management capabilities. Through meticulous development and integration, the app delivers a seamless and secure platform for food ordering and management.
